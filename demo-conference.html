About

The Annual St.Rose Market Day

Welcome to our exciting event that promises a day filled with joy and entertainment! Immerse yourself in an extraordinary experience as we combine the elements of a fun-walk, a bustling market, and captivating live performances. Join us for a delightful fun-walk that caters to individuals of all ages and fitness levels. Embark on a leisurely stroll through picturesque paths, taking in the beauty of our surroundings while fostering a sense of community and well-being. Whether you're a seasoned walker or prefer a more relaxed pace, our event offers the perfect opportunity to enjoy the outdoors, meet new people, and create lasting memories.

Indulge your senses at our vibrant market, where an assortment of stalls awaits your exploration. Discover an array of culinary delights from various cuisines, tantalizing your taste buds with flavors both local and international. Explore the vibrant displays of handcrafted jewelry, clothing, artwork, and unique treasures, lovingly crafted by talented artisans. Our market stalls provide a haven for those seeking one-of-a-kind finds or simply a delightful browsing experience. Prepare to be captivated by live performances that will ignite your passion for music, dance, and entertainment. Take a seat and be enthralled by talented artists who will grace the stage, captivating you with their electrifying performances and mesmerizing choreography. From soulful singers to dynamic dance troupes, our lineup offers something for everyone, ensuring an unforgettable experience that will leave you inspired and uplifted. Mark your calendar for this extraordinary event that seamlessly blends fun, culture, and community. Join us as we embark on a journey of togetherness, laughter, and discovery. We look forward to welcoming you and creating cherished memories at our upcoming event.
